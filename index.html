About me

My passion for quantitative problem solving started at the University of Technology of Troyes where I was fascinated to discover that mathematics and ant colony's pheromone-based communication together, gave birth to ACO, one of the most effective algorithms in computer science. I then graduated with a Master of Engineering in Computer Science, where I had dedicated myself to Operations Research and was fortunate enough to be mentored by some of the greatest minds in this field (coursework descrition).

I then studied towards a Master of Science in Management and Business Analytics at Ivey Business School, the second largest producer of business cases in the world, just behind Harvard (coursework descrition). The case-based learning method is an amazing experience that has expanded my knowledge in business key areas and unique characteristics of various industries. Hailing from France, I am a native French speaker, employing my technical and soft skills at leading companies like Mars and SAS Institute on operations research and data science. I am now working in Toronto, Canada at Data SEA Consulting as a Data Analytics Consultant.

Having experiences ranging from France to Canada, I have always stayed consistent in loving to learn, whether that be about data solutions or about the secrets of healthy living. I found myself very interested in reading online articles about artificial intelligence and emerging technologies as well as books related to self-help, psychology and communication.

Expertise

Data Engineering: I engineer end-to-end data products, from custom API clients and ingestion connectors to architecting Spark-based ETL pipelines within the Databricks Data Lakehouse Platform. I adhere to industry-leading DataOps standards, ensuring robust database management and data governance. My work guarantees efficient data flow and integrity, empowering organizations with reliable and scalable data infrastructure.

Machine Learning Engineering: I establish robust MLOps frameworks to streamline continuous training workflows and optimize the deployment of machine learning solutions. By serving models via production-grade API endpoints and ensuring continuous monitoring, I maintain high performance and reliability in our deployed solutions, enhancing the overall impact of machine learning applications.

Data Science: I leverage advanced data science methodologies to build various types of predictive and prescriptive models. My expertise lies in integrating different tools and frameworks at each step of the data science and model lifecycle, ensuring seamless development. This guarantees highly accuracate models, providing actionable insights and measurable improvements in business performance.

Software Engineering: I design CI/CD pipelines and automation strategies to foster DevOps adoption, resulting in enhanced team agility and accelerated software delivery cycles. My efforts focus on enhancing code robustness and feature reliability, ensuring seamless integration and deployment processes that drive consistent and high-quality software development.

Data Analysis: I harness the power of advanced analytics solutions to help clients solve complex problems and drive business outcomes. By promoting self-service data marts, automating reporting and operational processes, and delivering ad-hoc analysis, I enable organizations to make informed, data-driven decisions with greater efficiency and accuracy.
